Data Mining The Dark Web

About this project

Apart from the “visible”, surface web a “deep” and darker web does exist. The dark web relies in the combined availability of crypto currencies and anonymisation to work as a market place for drugs, documents, weapons and illegal items in general.

The dark web has been under scrutiny from legal authorities – both in the US and in other countries, with mixed success: while many web sites have been shut down, other resurface soon after.

In this Research Project we attempt to gain insight on this markets using a number of data mining and data analysis techniques and software packages

We hope the outcomes of this research to support legal authorities on their war against this dark markets as well as anyone with interest in the area

Timeline of the Dark Web

  • February 2011-October 2013

    The Silk Road

    It was the first dark web market place for illegal products to be established. With the combined efforts of FBI and EUROPOL the site was closed in 2011 but was later re-established online to be seized once again in 2013

  • September 2014

    Operation Onymous

    In the context of the largest operation against the dark web markets, a number of international law enforcement agencies, led by the FBI seized over 400 sites.

  • February 2014 - January 2016

    The Rise of Agora

    It didn't take long until a new site took the throne of the Silk Road as the largest market place in the dark web.

  • Summer 2015

    Dr. Andres Baravalle starts the research programme to which this project belongs.

  • 2016

    The fake passports and IDs market on the dark web becomes increasingly relevant for the authorities after realizing that members of ISIS may be travelling around Europe with the help of these documents.

Project Findings

  1. The Drug listings cope over 80% of the total market
  2. USA, UK and Australia are home for most of the drug distribution chains
  3. The value of the drugs market over the period of 2 months was of £25 million. One can buy up to 10kg of Cocaine from a single operation
  4. Passports, European and American ID cards are on sale, completely customizable.
  5. Leads were found that suggest the existance of large criminal organizations operating behind the drugs market:
    • The 10% largest vendors are responsible for 90% of the market
    • Drugs are provided from a number of countries by the largest drug dealers
    • This group of vendors provide on average over 5 different drug categories
